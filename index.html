Formula: DCS static earnings = routes × stops/route × DCS static stop rate. DPD sliding earnings = routes × stops/route × DPD matrix rate. Driver rate is calculated automatically from NMW: duty hours × baked NMW hourly rate ÷ stops/route. Fuel cost / route = miles ÷ MPG × 4.546 × (diesel price in £/L). PPM = (fuel cost / route) ÷ miles × 100. Diesel price defaults to the latest UK average and can be edited manually.
